Welcome! Here is our Story...


NOVOCREW, a division of the Nautika Group, was founded in 2006 in Brunei. We have successfully established affiliations and working relationships with the Maritime, Oil and Gas Industries since 1990. The company serves its esteemed global with a strong of over 600 employees in the forefront. Providing a wide range of maritime support services as specialists in the oil and gas industry. We deliver effective and cost-efficient solutions to shipowners, charterers and managers, with competent crew for all types of vessels

We are committed in conducting our business in a safe and efficient manner; to produce a cost-effective and high quality service to our customer.

Key people, our way to ensure service..

All our key personnel and managers, at NOVOCREW have in excess of 20 years maritime and oil & gas industry experience, dedicated to serving the requirements of shipowners, operators, managers and oil majors. Whether your company requires long term solutions or short term emergency cover, we are here to help you with an efficient and value added service.

Our Services

Read our Complete Quality Policy?
  

Active Management

NOVOCREW is an active manager. We ensure full compliance throughout the engagement of all statutory and client requirements. Professional staff, work with the employees, on all aspects of vessel manning including: Recruitment, Training, Health and safety, Visa, transport and mobilisation, Certification and accreditation, Payroll, and, Insurance

We work actively with Crew Managers, Charterers and Shipowners to solve their crew requirements, offering individually tailored or comprehensive effective and cost-efficient solutions.

  

Boost Returns

With a fixed all-inclusive cost for your crew, it is easier to manage your operational costs, reducing overall costs and saving office staff, marine base and management, valuable time.

 

Opening New Doors

Saving you time and expense by assuming responsibility for all the issues and concerns associated with placing and retaining your crew. We provide you with a one-stop shop, where NOVOCREW staff provides for every aspect of your maritime crew.

Your company's management is free to deal with creating new business opportunities, rather than being tied down to issues associated with managing your vessels crew.

You are empowered to make more accurate cost estimates, increasing your competitiveness to attract more business and boost your financial returns.



Crew Management

At NOVOCREW, we personally manage every aspect of our clients requirements. We provide our clients with advance manpower and real-time cost analysis to ensure the highest-quality and most cost-effective solutions. And with our state-of-the-art MPS Crew Management System, NOVOCREW staff monitors all crew placements.

Insurance

Saving you precious time and the headache of complex insurance matters. We make sure all insurance requirements are in place and fully compliant. We insure the crew members under a Workers Compensation or equivalent scheme and guarantee that the benefits are equal to statutory requirements in the place of engagement.

Payroll

NOVOCREW takes care of Payroll requirements, no need to worry about paying the crew, we handle all payroll processing for you. We can also provide payroll forecasts to further enhance crew cost planning and budgeting.

Mobilisation

Our team ensures the prompt and fast mobilisation and de-mobilisation of crew. All Visas, work permits, accommodation and mobilisation costs are covered by NOVOCREW in one charge-off rate basis.

Recruitment

NOVOCREW conducts crew searches to find the best crew for specialized vessel operations. NOVOCREW checks, audits and analyses the training and qualifications of each employee to ensure they are competent and job-ready. We maintain real-time alerts for monitoring expiring travel documents and certificates of employees to guarantee conformance and smooth operation.
